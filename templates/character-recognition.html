Character Recognition

Cartoon character recognition known as character recognition is used to identify cartoon characters in an image. From a total of around 20 characters from the series, the correct one can be predicted using the image. Identify your character with just a click!

The Process

How it's done?

The image before passing on to the neural network, undergoes a preprocessing stage. The image is converted from BGR form to RGB and resized as a 100 x 100 pixel image. To classify the characters, a deep convolutional neural network consisting 7 convolutional layers divided into 4 Pooling layers and then the extracted features from the convolutional layer are passed as an input to a dense 2 layer neural network.

The dense neural network layers outputs probability for each character that could be in the image. charactes's class having the highest probability is taken as the predicted character.
